## Common questions

### Voice input isn't transcribing correctly

The voice pipeline understands Egyptian Arabic, English, and code-switched speech. For best results:

- Record in a quiet place.
- Hold the phone close to your mouth (15-20 cm).
- Speak naturally, not too fast. The app handles dialect — you don't need to use Modern Standard Arabic.
- Keep recordings under 60 seconds when possible.
- Avoid background music or video playing nearby (Whisper sometimes transcribes lyrics).

If transcription is consistently wrong on specific phrases, email us with the phrase you tried — we expand the Egyptian glossary weekly based on real user examples.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open **iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → sKedIt AI → Cancel Subscription**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Apple, not sKedIt, controls billing and refunds. For a refund, visit **https://reportaproblem.apple.com**.

### I hit my monthly free-tier limit. When does it reset?

Free-tier limits (5 voice, 5 smart schedule, 5 reschedules, 3 goal schedules) reset at the start of each calendar month in your local time zone. Or upgrade to Pro for unlimited usage — Profile → "Upgrade →".

### I changed my Apple ID and lost my Pro subscription

In the paywall screen, tap **"Restore purchases"** at the bottom. This re-links your existing Apple subscription to the app. If that doesn't work, email us with your purchase receipt (Apple emails you one for every renewal).

### My recurring blocks disappeared after about three months

This is expected behaviour, not a bug. Recurring blocks are pre-generated for a window of **90 days** from the moment you create the recurring task. Once that window closes, the series stops showing on the calendar.

To continue a recurring task, just re-create it — you'll get a fresh 90-day window. This deliberate finite window keeps your local data small and the calendar fast.

In-app, the **How to use** screen (Profile → Help → How to use sKedIt) covers this in detail.

### How do I delete my account and all my data?

In the app: **Profile → "Delete account & data"**. This wipes everything immediately — your local schedule, AI usage history, subscription record, and account email — via a server-side function. You'll be signed out and bounced to the login screen. Once confirmed, there's no way to recover the account.

If our server can't be reached at the moment you delete, the app falls back to a manual path and asks you to email us; we'll complete the deletion within 14 days.

### Does sKedIt store my voice recordings?

No. Audio is sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription, then discarded immediately. We never see the recording, and we never store it. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.html) for the full data-flow.

### My voice input was understood differently than I meant

Tap any of the cards on the confirmation screen to edit before saving — you can fix the title, time, area, recurrence, or duration before adding to your schedule. Or tap "Try again" to re-record.
