2. What the app does

sKedIt AI is an AI-powered scheduling assistant. It lets you:

  • Create schedule blocks, tasks, and goals by typing or speaking (Egyptian Arabic, English, or a mix).
  • Have AI parse your spoken instructions into structured calendar items.
  • Have AI suggest optimal placements, rescheduling options, and break long-term goals into focused sessions.
  • Track your daily completion streak and goal progress.

Voice and AI features rely on third-party AI providers (OpenAI for transcription, Anthropic Claude for parsing). The app is a productivity tool — not a medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical service.
