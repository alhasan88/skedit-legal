## 2. What the app does

sKedIt AI is an AI-powered scheduling assistant. It lets you:

- Create schedule blocks, tasks, and long-term goals by typing or by speaking (in Egyptian Arabic, English, or a mix).
- Have AI parse your spoken instructions into structured calendar items.
- Have AI suggest optimal placements, rescheduling options, and break-up long-term goals into focused sessions.
- Track your daily completion streak and goal progress.

Voice features rely on third-party AI APIs (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI Whisper). The app is intended as a productivity tool — it is not a medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical service.
